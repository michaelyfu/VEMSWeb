ABOUT THE APP

Overview

A Volunteer Management System that verifies the participation of individuals at events they have signed up for by organizing their engagement hours

What it does

Allows administrators to generate barcodes that are then scanned by volunteers at the events to validate check-in

Why it matters

Automates the volunteer-check in process to ensure all hours are accurate
